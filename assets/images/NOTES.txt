This folder is where your image files live: logo, photos, the white paper cover, social
share image, anything else the site references.

Suggested files to add once you have them:
- logo.svg or logo.png (referenced from the header if you want a mark instead of, or
  alongside, the text logo)
- og-image.jpg (the image shown when the site is shared on LinkedIn or elsewhere,
  1200x630px is the standard size)
- white-paper-cover.jpg (if you want a cover image on the white paper page)

You can delete this file once you've added real images. It's excluded from the
published site either way, since it has no front matter for Jekyll to process.
