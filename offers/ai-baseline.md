<div class="eyebrow">Who it's for</div>

Leaders who want an honest, low cost read on AI readiness before committing to a larger piece of work, or who aren't yet sure whether the problem is big enough to warrant one.

<div class="eyebrow">What's in the report</div>

- A rating for each of the six domains
- The specific findings behind each rating, not generic commentary
- A clear view of which domain to look at first, and why

<div class="eyebrow">How it works</div>

<ol class="offer-steps">
  <li>Complete an 18-question assessment online. Takes about 15 to 20 minutes.</li>
  <li>Your responses are scored across six domains, operating model, governance, data quality, decision rights, leadership and delivery maturity, using a consistent rating logic.</li>
  <li>You receive a written report within five working days.</li>
</ol>

<div class="eyebrow">What this isn't</div>

There are no interviews and no organisational mapping. It's a fast, honest gut check, not a transformation plan. If the report surfaces something serious, the natural next step is the [AI Readiness Diagnostic]({{ '/ai-readiness-diagnostic/' | relative_url }}).
